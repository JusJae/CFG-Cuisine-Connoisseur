Stoke-On-Trent
The Potteries
Home of the true Staffordshire oatcake

In Stoke-On-Trent you'll struggle to find someone who doesn't recommend heading down to the local oatcake shop as a great way to start the day. Whether you're a lover of a simple sausage and cheese oatcake, or a hearty full English on an oatcake, you'll certainly find a filling to enjoy!





Best Oatcakes in Staffordshire

Castle Oatcakes

While technically just outside of Stoke-on-Trent in Newcastle-under-Lyme one of the most loved oatcake shops is Castle Oatcakes. For fans of "World's Strongest Man" Castle Oatcakes is home of the Eddie Hall Special. A real gut buster that's for sure!

Hole in the Wall Oatcakes

Known by locals as the best in the Biz is the Hole In The Wall oatcake shop in Burlsem. Starting life as a simple oatcake shop based from someones home, they now have a number of stores across the city that still offer the home cooked quality!

Oatcakes and Milkshakes

A recent addition to the area is Oatcakes and Milkshakes (Based in Stone). Here you can enjoy all the classic oatcake fillings, but have them with a monster of a milkshake on the side. Which fillings will you pair with a tasty milkshake?
